Glossary for Stakeholders

  • Precision: How many of the extracted items were actually correct? (No noise)
  • Recall: Did we find all the items that were supposed to be there? (No missing data)
  • F1 Score: The balance between Precision and Recall.
  • TSR (Table Structure Recognition): Overall pass/fail based on a 90% Recall threshold.
